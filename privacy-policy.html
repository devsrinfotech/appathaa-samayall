Privacy Policy

At Appathaa Samayall, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. We value the trust you place in us and are committed to transparently communicating how we collect, process, and utilise your data.

How We Use Your Information

We collect information to enhance the functionality and user experience of our website. This includes notifying you about service updates, changes in our offerings, and any improvements to our website structure.

Your data also enables us to promote our diverse range of cuisines and services, ensuring you stay informed about our latest offerings and features.
All information collected is utilised in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Collection Methods

We employ lawful and legitimate means to gather information, such as when you inquire about our services through various channels. This data helps us tailor customised proposals to meet your culinary preferences and requirements.

Additionally, information voluntarily provided by you through our website or other communication channels is collected to facilitate seamless interaction and to improve your overall experience.
Cookies are utilised to optimise website navigation and maintain continuity during your visit.

Safety of Personal Information

Rest assured, your personal information is securely stored and protected against theft, loss, unauthorised access, or misuse. We do not sell your data to third parties without your explicit consent.

Information Disclosure

We are committed to upholding the confidentiality of your data. However, there are limited circumstances where we may disclose information

  1. Legal Compliance: If required by law enforcement authorities, we may be obligated to provide information.

  2. Business Transfers: In the event of selling our business or a portion of it, information may be shared to facilitate the transition.

Updates to Privacy Policy

Any amendments to our privacy policy will be communicated to you through various channels and will be promptly updated on this page.

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we strive to ensure the protection and security of your personal information at all times.

For any queries or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us

Thank you for choosing Appathaa Samayall. We look forward to serving you with delicious culinary delights while respecting your privacy preferences.
